Generate fixed links for specific map positions and view states (position, scale, rotation), then navigate directly from external documents (Excel, PDF) to precise map locations with a single click. Version 1.2.0 adds explicit support for map rotation via a `rotation` parameter and standardizes on `scale` as the preferred sizing parameter (with optional `zoom` provided for Google Maps compatibility). Improvements also include refined zoom estimation for Google Maps and more robust handling of projected CRSs.
